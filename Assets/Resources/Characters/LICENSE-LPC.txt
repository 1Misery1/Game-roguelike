Modifications made to the original
----------------------------------
- Removed the original background (alpha keyed out).
- Cropped individual standing/idle poses out of the full LPC animation sheets.
- Re-packed selected frames into per-character horizontal sprite sheets.
- Squared the canvas and upscaled 4x with nearest-neighbour (see
  extract_sprites.py / _RawSprites/fix_sprites.py at the repo root).
